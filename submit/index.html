  • ALL SUBMISSIONS MUST BE EMAILED TO SUBMISSIONS@LAUTREMOT.COM WITH THE SUBJECT "SUBMISSION: [GENRE(S) OF YOUR PIECE(S)] [YOUR NAME]"

  • L'AUTRE MOT is a Montréal-based literary magazine. We will be publishing biannually a SPRING and FALL issue. Submissions are ALWAYS OPEN. We want work from writers and artists ANYWHERE IN THE WHOLE WORLD, and, perhaps less realistically, in ANY LANGAUGE.

  • You may submit any combination of fiction, poetry, nonfiction, art, or photography, but no more than 2 OF EACH. Poetry and fiction are greatly preferred. Especially poetry. Work of any length is permitted, but shorter is better.
  • Writing must be submitted as a PDF.
  • Visual art must be submitted as a PNG.
  • Include in your email a cover letter and a brief, third-person bio that begins with the name you would like to be credited under
  • As with essentially any literary magazine, simultaneous submissions are fine, as long as you notify us if your work has been accepted anywhere else.
  • Any work that has been previously published anywhere else will be rejected.
  • Any work produced with AI in any way will be rejected.
  • If you are in the Montréal area, and would like to bias me in favor of your work, you are more than welcome to buy me a glass of wine at a bar of your choosing (within reason). Simply mention so in your email.
